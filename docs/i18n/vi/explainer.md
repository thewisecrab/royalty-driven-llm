# Giai thich RDLLM

## ELI5

Hay xem cau tra loi AI nhu bai bao cao o truong. RDLLM bat AI cho biet da dung
sources nao, cau nao duoc source nao support, va ai nen nhan credit hoac payment.

## Simple

RDLLM them source footer hien thi vao cau tra loi AI. No kiem tra sources co
support claims hay khong va noi visible source usage voi payout hoac escrow.

## Non-Technical

Users thay sources va Claim Evidence. Creators co attribution evidence.
Operators co gates truoc khi hien thi cau tra loi nhu grounded hoac
royalty-bearing.

## Technical

RDLLM tao source rows va Claim Evidence rows voi claim hash, support score,
evidence span hash, warrant status, source-disagreement status va payout
allocation. Verifiers tinh lai hashes, citations, links, metrics,
attribution-gap closure va `source_grounding_acceptance`.
